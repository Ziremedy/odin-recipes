Tabbouleh


Tabbouleh, aka tabouli, is a fresh herb and bulgur Mediterranean salad, with parsley being the main ingredient instead of the traditional lettuce.
It's dotted with diced cucumber and tomato, and dressed with olive oil and lemon juice. It's refreshing, light and packed with healthy ingredients.

Time: 40 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • Chopped parsley (4 Cups)
  • Lemon juice (6 tbsp)
  • Extra fine bulgur (1/2 cup)
  • Firm tomatoes (2-4)
  • Green onions whole (4)
  • Mint leaves (1/2 cup) optional
  • Extra virgin olive oil (2/3 cup)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, whisk olive oil and lemon juice together until well combined, then add the bulgur to the dressing and let it soak until it is soft and plumped, should be about 15 minutes.
  2. Prepare the vegetables by washing and drying first, then finely chop. When preparing the tomatoes, make sure to drain any excess juice.
  3. Place the chopped vegetables in a large bowl, and add the optional mint. Season with salt and pepper, then pour the bulgur and dressing mixture over. Gently toss.
  4. All done! It is best served cold, and with lettuce if desired. Enjoy.
